Pricing FAQ

Do you charge per webhook or per rebook?

No. The monthly subscription covers everything — unlimited webhook fires, unlimited PRISM evaluations. You pay a flat fee regardless of how many signals you receive or rebooks you execute.

Why start with the free plan?

It's the full product, capped at 5 bookings. Run it on your highest-value bookings first to verify the signals work end-to-end in your environment before scaling up.

What happens if I exceed my booking limit?

New submissions return a 402 with a clear error message. Your existing monitored bookings continue uninterrupted. Upgrade your plan to add more capacity.

What counts as an "active" booking?

Any booking currently being monitored by PRISM (submitted and not yet departed or cancelled). Bookings are automatically retired after the departure date.

Is there an annual billing option?

Not yet — monthly only for now. We'll add annual billing with a discount once we exit beta.
