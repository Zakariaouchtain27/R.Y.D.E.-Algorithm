Questions

What if prices never drop?

You pay absolutely nothing. We only earn when we actually save you money.

Will I get the same seat?

We rebook the same cabin class on the same route. The specific seat may change. You can set "same seat preferred" when registering.

How do you access my booking?

We integrate with airlines via Duffel's NDC API. Your booking reference lets us cancel and rebook on your behalf.

Is my payment safe?

Yes. Payments are processed by Stripe. We never store your card details — only a secure token used to charge the success fee.
